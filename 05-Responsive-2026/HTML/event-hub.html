Upcoming Events

Sunday Funday

From May 1st until September 30th, we will be playing boardgames and eat the famous bomboloni at cafe La Pagoda in the Pineta di Ponente, Viareggio, each Sunday.
Bring your own games or play ours.
All LUMA members are welcome and each member can bring 1 non-member.
We start at 12.00 and finish around 20.00.

PLAY, The Games Festival

From May 4th to 6th, 2025, Bologna hosts the 16th edition of PLAY, the biggest Italian convention dedicated to games of any kind.
This year's program includes events for anyone and any age: concerts, cosplay contests, photo exhibits, workshops, themed “escape games”, VR games, meeting with artists, illustrators, developers and much more.
Your boardgame club LUMA will attend!

Carcassonne Competition

Carcassonne with its endless expansions remains one of the most   - if not the most -   popular tile-placement games.
After 4 time-consuming elimination rounds, we could finally crown our winner. Our winner had to fight against 49 other players and sleep.
It was an exhausting, but above all fun and successful event.
